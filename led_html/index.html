Animated Led Board

  • Easy to use: just 1 Click actions!
  • No mask or selection needed: click the layer, choose the action and play it
  • Non-destructive, keeps the original images untouched
  • Four led sizes:
    • X-Small = ⌀ 8px
    • Small = ⌀ 18px
    • Medium = ⌀ 28px
    • Large = ⌀ 38px
  • 10 animation frames
  • Background on a separate Color Fill Layer
  • Also works with clipped images, shapes and text layers

Folder content:

  • 1 Action's set file: LED_ANIMATION.atn
  • 1 Brushes set file: LED_BRUSHES.abr
  • 1 PDF Help File
  • 1 TEST-FILE.psd

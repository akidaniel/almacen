Retro Mosaic

  • Easy to use: just 1 Click actions!
  • No mask or selection needed: open the image and play the action
  • Non-destructive, keeps the original images untouched
  • 12 different tile designs

  • 5 sizes tiles: 20, 30, 40, 50 and 60 px
  • = 36 total actions

Install:

Load the Retro Mosaic Actions

  • GEOMETRIC_WIPE_03_SMALL.atn
  • GEOMETRIC_WIPE_03_MEDIUM.atn
  • GEOMETRIC_WIPE_03_BIG.atn

Load the Retro Mosaic Tiles Brushes

  • RETRO_MOSAIC.abr

Use:

  • Open the image
  • Choose a foreground color for the darker tiles
  • Choose a background color for the lighter tiles
  • from the Actions Panel select the action size
  • Play the action
  • The action ends with a New Layer Group named as the action played containing all the tiles

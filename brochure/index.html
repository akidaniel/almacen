Vector People

Features:
  • Optimized design for CC 2018 and plus and adapted for previous versions (from CS6 to CC2017)
  • Well arranged layout in four layers:
    • Images Top
    • Text
    • Images Bottom
    • Background
  • Well arranged text styles
  • Modular Product Rows
    Customize the layout of your design using one, two, three, four, five or six products per row on each page
  • Main design colors easily customizable
  • Prices with GREP Styles optimized for $ and € wich includes:
    • Auto superscript in cents
    • Auto hidden ",00" cents
    • Auto percentage style after the price
    • Auto superscript to the $ symbol
    • Auto subscript to the € symbol
    • Auto negative indentation to the € symbol (offer price)
  • Two different prices styles in two sizes
    • Old Price Small
    • Old Price Big
    • Price Small
    • Price Big

  • 32 Color Bullets Styles
